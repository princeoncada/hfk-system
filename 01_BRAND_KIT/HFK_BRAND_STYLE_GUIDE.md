# HFK BRAND STYLE GUIDE

## Visual Direction
- Cozy
- Gentle
- Homeschool-friendly
- Hand-crafted feel

## Texture Style
- Colored pencil
- Soft grain
- Slight paper warmth

## Typography
- Rounded playful headers
- Neat handwritten body text

## Layout Rules
- Spacious
- Printable
- Structured sections
- Doodle-supported

## Avoid
- Sharp modern corporate design
- Empty layouts
- Overly glossy renders
- AI-looking compositions
