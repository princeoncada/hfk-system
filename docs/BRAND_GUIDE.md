# HFK Brand Guide

## Identity

Homeschooling for Kiddos (HFK) is a warm, encouraging Facebook educational
page for homeschool families and parents teaching Grades 1–6.

## Emotional Direction

Target feeling:
- Warm and cozy
- Playful and inviting
- Parent-friendly and child-friendly
- Encouraging without pressure
- Educational without overwhelm

Avoid:
- Corporate or clinical appearance
- Sterile generic worksheet factory aesthetics
- Glossy AI-generated visuals
- Overdesigned or overly polished layouts
- Cold, institutional typography

## Visual Inspiration

- Homeschool activity books
- Hand-crafted printable learning pages
- Colored-pencil warmth
- Child-loved, worn-in layouts
- Simple doodle decorations

## Color Direction

Primary palette (warm and earthy):
- Warm cream / off-white backgrounds
- Soft yellow accents
- Muted sage green
- Dusty rose / soft coral
- Warm brown for text and borders

Avoid: harsh primaries, neon, high-contrast corporate palettes.

## Typography Direction

- Friendly, rounded sans-serif for headings
- Readable serif or clean sans for body text
- Avoid ultra-thin or overly geometric typefaces
- Print-friendly at standard sizes

## Tone of Voice

- Warm and encouraging
- Direct and clear for instructions
- Celebratory for achievements
- Never condescending to parents or children
- Bible/Values content: reverent but accessible

## Subjects

- Math (numeracy, operations, problem solving)
- Science (nature, basic concepts, curiosity-driven)
- Reading + Vocabulary (phonics, comprehension, word work)
- Bible / Values (character, stories, reflection)
