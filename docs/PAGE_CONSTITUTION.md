# HFK Page Constitution

## Mission

Homeschooling for Kiddos (HFK) exists to make homeschooling feel
accessible, warm, and achievable for everyday families.

We create practical educational content that parents can trust and
children can enjoy — printable, shareable, and grounded in real
learning goals.

## Audience

Primary: Parents and homeschool families
Learners: Grades 1–6 (ages 6–12)

## Content Pillars

Math
  Numeracy, operations, patterns, problem solving.
  Approachable. No intimidation. Build confidence first.

Science
  Nature, basic concepts, curiosity-driven exploration.
  Wonder over memorization.

Reading + Vocabulary
  Phonics, comprehension, word work, early literacy.
  Encouraging, not pressuring.

Bible / Values
  Character development, Bible stories, reflection prompts.
  Reverent, accessible, never heavy-handed.

## Publishing Principles

1. Every piece of content serves the learner, not the algorithm.
2. Printable content must be usable without internet.
3. Captions should feel like a note from a helpful friend.
4. We do not publish content that wasn't reviewed by a human operator.
5. Brand identity (avatars, visual style) is consistent and deliberate.

## Content Calendar Philosophy

Content is planned, not reactive.
Ideas enter the backlog before they become posts.
Grade-appropriate targeting is required for every piece.
Subject rotation keeps the feed balanced.

## Voice Guidelines

- Write as a supportive homeschool parent, not a textbook.
- Celebrate effort and curiosity.
- Use inclusive language (families, parents, guardians).
- Bible/Values content: warm and welcoming, never exclusionary.
- Keep captions scannable — busy parents read fast.
